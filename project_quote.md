## Project Overview
Design, development, and deployment of a professional, mobile-responsive corporate website for **Shaik & Reddy Associates** to establish a strong digital presence, highlight financial services, and generate client leads.

## Scope of Work

### 1. UI/UX Design & Frontend Development
*   **Custom Landing Page:** High-conversion, premium single-page design highlighting services (GST, ITR, Bookkeeping, etc.) and the "Why Choose Us" value propositions.
*   **Mobile Responsiveness:** Fluid layouts optimized for all screen sizes (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop) using Bootstrap 5.
*   **Modern Animations:** Scroll-reveal animations (AOS) for a dynamic and engaging user experience.
*   **Contact Page:** Dedicated page featuring location details, integrated WhatsApp quick-links, and a fully functional lead capture form.

### 2. Contact Form & Email Integration
*   **EmailJS Integration:** Secure, serverless configuration to instantly route all contact form submissions directly to `Shaikandreddy@gmail.com`.
*   Form validation to ensure required fields (Name, Email) are captured accurately.

### 3. Deployment & Hosting Setup
*   **Vercel Deployment:** Production-ready deployment on Vercel's global edge network for lightning-fast loading speeds and high uptime.
*   SSL Certificate (HTTPS) provisioning for secure browsing.
